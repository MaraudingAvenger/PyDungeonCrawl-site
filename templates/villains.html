Lost Kobold

The Lost Kobold is a small, timid creature that has been separated from its tribe. It is weak in combat, but makes up for it with its ability to cry and make you feel bad.

Fumbling Attack Clumsily swipe at the enemy and attempt to harm them.

Kobold Mother

The Kobold Mother is a fierce creature that fiercely guards her young. She is a formidable opponent in combat, wielding a crude but deadly weapon, and using her ferocity to her advantage. She is much larger and stronger than the Lost Kobold and is able to take more damage. Her fighting style is more aggressive, she will relentlessly pursue the player and will not hesitate to attack them if they are seen as a threat to her offspring. The Kobold Mother is a formidable enemy for the player to face early on, and serves as a reminder that the player's actions have consequences.

Motherly Love A fierce attack dealing enough damage to require your healers attention

Kobold Queen

The Kobold Queen is the leader of the kobold tribe and commands great respect from her subjects. She is a formidable opponent in combat, wielding a powerful weapon and using her mastery of dark magic to curse her foes. Her attacks are swift and savage, and her strikes are capable of dealing massive damage to the player. She is also able to apply a curse to the player, which will deal damage over time and can only be cured before time runs out. The Kobold Queen is a formidable enemy that will test the player's combat skills and strategic thinking, as they must find a way to cure the curse and defeat her in battle. The player must be careful, for the Queen is more powerful than the previous kobolds, and will not hesitate to kill them with her curse if they are not well prepared.

Savage Strike A fierce attack dealing enough damage to require your healers attention

Curse A deadly curse and damage over time ability that will kill the player unless they receive a Cure by the party healer.

Kobold Goddess

The Kobold Goddess is the ultimate leader of the kobold tribe and the embodiment of their dark powers. She is an imposing figure, larger and more powerful than any of her subjects. She wields a formidable weapon and uses dark magic to curse her foes, similar to the kobold queen, but with even more devastating effects. Her attacks are swift and savage, capable of dealing massive damage to the player. She also has the ability to devour the player's soul, which will instantly kill them if they don't move out of the way. The player must be extremely careful when facing the Kobold Goddess, as her abilities are far more powerful than any they have faced before and they will need to use all their skills and knowledge to defeat her.

Savage Strike A fierce attack dealing enough damage to require your healers attention

Curse A deadly curse and damage over time ability that will kill the player unless they receive a Cure by the party healer.

Devour Souls The Kobold Goddess will devour your soul if you remain still on the battlefield. You better watch out for the telegraph to see when this is happening!

Savage Mountain Troll

The Savage Mountain Troll is a massive and fearsome creature that dominates the forest. Standing at an imposing height, the troll is a formidable opponent for the players. It has the ability of self-regeneration, which makes it hard to defeat, and is able to deal massive damage with its single-target attack. The Savage Mountain Troll also has a powerful ability called "Colossal Smash" where it jumps into the air and smashes the ground, causing widespread damage to the surrounding area. Additionally, it can throw boulders at a target position, which can be a deadly surprise for the players. To make things worse, if there is no enemy in melee range, the Savage Mountain Troll can charge towards the nearest target with its death charge. The Savage Mountain Troll is a formidable enemy that requires careful planning and execution to defeat. The players must use all the skills and resources at their disposal to defeat the creature and emerge victorious.

Self Regeneration The troll will slowly Heal over time

Decimate A fierce single target attack that will challenge your healers.

Roar of Giants The troll lets out a roar that debilitates your party and applies 10 stacks of Frailty.

Colossal Smash The troll jumps into the air smashing the ground dealing damage to the entire party.

Throw Bolder A telegraphed attack where the troll will identify the furthest target and throw a bolder at their position.

Death Charge If no target is in melee range of the troll for 2 turns it will charge at the nearest target and kill them.
