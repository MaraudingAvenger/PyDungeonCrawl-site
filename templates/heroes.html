Guardian

The Guardian is a powerful and experienced tank, known for their mastery of defensive combat techniques. They are exceptionally durable and able to withstand a great deal of damage, able to strike the target, reduce the damage they receive, reflect damage, stun the target, and redirect damage they receive to heal the party and apply toughness to them.

Defensive Strike Strike the target and apply 2 stacks of Toughness to yourself.

Shield Stance Take on a defensive stance reducing damage you receive by 75% for 3 turns but the Guardian cannot take any action during this time. At the end you will receive 5 stacks of Toughness for each hit taken.

Shield Spike Give yourself 50% Reflect for 2 turns. Consumes your Toughness and adds it to the Reflect amount.

Shield Bash Bash the target with your shield and apply Stun for 1 turn.

Inspiration All damage the Guardian receives will instead Heal the Party and apply 1 stack of Toughness for 5 turns.

Paladin

The Paladin is a powerful holy warrior tank, balanced between defensive and offensive capabilities. They can strike the target with holy damage and apply frailty, consume frailty to increase their own toughness, create barriers to absorb and store damage, empower allies next attack with their toughness and trigger a holy nova to deal damage and heal the party while refreshing their own toughness.

Holy Strike Strike the target and apply 1 stack of Frailty that lasts 5 turns.

Exorcise Excise the target and consume all stacks of Frailty to give yourself double the stacks in Toughness for 3 turns.

Divine Protection Apply a Barrier to target or self to absorb and store the damage. Once this barrier absorbs damage it will expire on that turn. Lasts up to 10 turns or until damage received.

Prayer Consume your Toughness stacks and buff the Party increasing the power of their NextAttack by the percentage of Toughness consumed.

Holy Nova Damage absorbed by Divine Protection's Barrier is used to trigger a holy nova on your target dealing damage and will also Heal all Party members. Refreshes your Toughness stacks and applies your Toughness stacks to the group for 5 turns.

Berzerker

The Berzerker is a savage fighter who is focused solely on destruction. They are known for their relentless and brutal fighting style, often disregarding their own defense in order to deal as much damage as possible to their enemies. As they fight, they become increasingly bloodthirsty, losing themselves in battle and becoming a force of destruction on the battlefield. They are skilled in various forms of melee combat and have little regard for their own safety, making them a formidable opponent to anyone who stands in their way. Berzerkers are often seen as wild and uncontrollable, driven by their desire for violence and destruction.

Charge Instantly move yourself to the front of the target.

Frenzied Attack Strike the target and apply 2 stacks of Might and ExposeWeakness to self that last until removed.

Rampage A devastating attack that applies 8 stacks of Might and 4 ExposeWeakness to yourself that last until removed but cripples the enemy and applies 4 stacks of MagicVulnerability, ExposeWeakness, and also PoisonVulnerability.

Parry Gain the Parry effect and remove all stacks of Might and ExposeWeakness on self. If you are attacked this turn receive 2 stacks of Toughness. Successfully parrying an attack triggers 4 stacks of ExposeWeakness on the target for 3 turns.

Battle Shout Party receives all of your Might stacks for 10 turns.

Cleric

The Cleric class is a supportive class that specializes in healing and defensive abilities. They have the ability to instantly heal allies, apply stuns, deal damage with a debuff, remove negative effects and create a barrier that absorbs damage and empowers their next attack.

Healing Word Instantly Heal the target for 50% health which scales further with buffs and equipment.

Jolt Apply Stun on the target for 2 turns. 3 turns if the target has MagicVulnerability

Smite Smite the target and apply 1 stack of Frailty. Damage absorbed by Group Barrier is added and triggers 5 additional stacks of Frailty.

Cleanse Casts Cure on Target

Group Barrier Create a Barrier that absorbs 100% of damage received for 4 turns. All damage absorbed is used to empower your next Smite.

Druid

The Druid class is a versatile class that specializes in nature magic providing both healing and crowd control abilities. They have the ability to heal allies, root enemies, apply debuffs, remove negative effects and create a shield that reflects damage and increases party's resistance for a certain duration.

Swift Mend Instantly Heal the target for 30% and then an additional 30% over 2 turns which scales further with buffs and equipment.

Grasping Roots Casts Root on target that lasts 4 turns. Also applies 5 stacks of ExposeWeakness if target has MagicVulnerability

Blaze Blasts the target with intense heat and apply 1 stack of ExposeWeakness and FireResistance

Salve Casts Cure on Target

Group Thornshield Apply Reflect and 5 stacks of Toughness to Party for 8 turns

Shaman

The Shaman class is a versatile class that specializes in spiritual magics providing both healing and buffing allies, as well as debuffing enemies. They have the ability to heal allies over time, apply poison to enemies, remove negative effects , and increase the physical power of the entire party.

Regenerate Apply a Heal over time to the target for 80% health over 5 turns which scales further with buffs and equipment.

Enhancement Apply 5 stacks of Might and Toughness to the target for 3 turns

Poison Frost Apply a frost based poison to the target and 2 stacks of Frailty for 4 turns. Also applies 1 stack of FrostResistance

Purge Casts Cure on Target

Group Infusion Apply 10 stacks of Might to Party for 8 turns

Bard

The Bard is a versatile musician who uses their music to empower allies and defeat enemies. They can heal party members, provide powerful buffs, remove negative effects, and deal magic damage with their abilities. Their ultimate ability, Crescendo, combines all of their abilities into one powerful song that boosts their allies and damages enemies. With their musical talents, the Bard can be a valuable asset in any party.

Healing Notes All Party members Heal for 10% of their health.

Violent Notes All Party members receive 5 stacks of Might.

Curative Notes All Party members will receive Cure in 2 turns.

Magical Note Play a piercing magical note at the enemy that deals Magic damage and applies 4 stacks of ExposeWeakness. Increased to 6 stacks and applies Stun if the target has MagicVulnerability.

Crescendo Combine all your notes into a chaotic lullaby that triggers all bard songs and the NextAttack for all group members will do double damage.

Ranger

The Ranger is a long-range archer who supports allies with damage, debuffs, and minor healing. They can fire arrows from anywhere in the arena, dealing increased damage the further away they are while applying stacks of Expose Weakness to the target. They also have abilities that apply Poison and Magic vulnerability to the target, making them more vulnerable to damage. The Ranger also has the ability to support allies with instant healing and removing negative effects. With their superior ranged abilities and support skills, the Ranger is a valuable asset in any party.

Shoot Fire an arrow from anywhere in the arena. Damage increased by distance. 3 shots in a row will apply 1 stack of ExposeWeakness for 2 turns.

Virulent Arrow Fire a Poison arrow. If the target is not currently affected by a poison, virulent arrow applies PoisonVulnerability for 3 turns. Counts as a shot but does not apply ExposeWeakness.

Frostfire Arrow Fire an elemental arrow. If the target does not have FireResistance or FrostResistance, frostfire arrow applies 3 stacks of MagicVulnerability. Counts as a shot but does not apply ExposeWeakness.

Field Medicine Instantly Heal the target for 20% and apply Cure.

Murder Send a murder of crows to claw at the target defenses applying Blind and 6 stacks of ExposeWeakness for 4 turns.

Rogue

The Rogue is a deadly assassin who excels in surprise attacks and quick kills. They can deal double damage and apply debuffs when attacking from behind, apply poison and make the enemy frail, blind the target, and increase the stacks of expose weakness and magic vulnerability on the target. Their ultimate ability, Ambush, allows them to deal massive damage and apply various debuffs when attacking from behind. With their deadly skills and tactics, the Rogue is a formidable force in combat.

Backstab Stab the target. Deals double damage when used from behind. 3 double damage backstabs in a row will apply 2 stacks of ExposeWeakness to the target for 2 turns.

Envenom Apply poison to the target and 4 stacks of Frailty for 4 turns. Does not count as a backstab.

Sand Apply Blind the target this turn.

Shank Stab the target and twist the blade. Doubles the stacks of ExposeWeakness for 2 turns and doubles MagicVulnerability on the target. Does not count as a backstab.

Ambush If behind the target provides a powerful 10x damage backstab that applies Blind for 2 turns and PoisonVulnerability plus 4 stacks of Frailty for 10 turns. Counts as a normal backstab

Wizard

The Wizard is a master of the arcane, able to deliver powerful magical attacks and empower them with fire and frost magic. They can blast the target with magic missiles and weave fire and frost magic to create Magic Vulnerability on their enemies. They also have the ability to teleport themselves or allies to anywhere on the battlefield. Their ultimate ability is a powerful lightning strike that deals extra damage and applies additional debuffs if the target already has Magic Vulnerability. The Wizard is a formidable force on the battlefield.

Magic Missile Blast the target with magic missiles! If the target has MagicVulnerability damage is increased to 100 and applies Stun for 1 turn.

Fire Bolt Blast the target with a ball of Fire and apply 2 stacks of FireResistance.

Frost Bolt Blast the target with a ball of Frost and apply 2 stacks of FrostResistance.

Teleport Instantly Teleport self or target to anywhere in the arena.

Lightning Strike Call down a powerful lightning strike on the target! If the target has MagicVulnerability this deals double damage, applies Stun, and 5 stacks of MagicVulnerability on the target for 3 turns.

Necromancer

The Necromancer is a dark master of blood magic, death, and the undead, using their powers to sacrifice themselves and allies to deal damage and cripple enemies. They can blast the target with poison, apply negative effects and delayed damage, sacrifice health for damage, leach health from the target, and summon shades of death to infect the target's soul with a crippling poison. With their dark powers, the Necromancer is a formidable force on the battlefield.

Poison Blast Blast the target with Poison waves of pain and apply 1 stack of ExposeWeakness for 4 turns.

Doom Consume and combine the negative effects on the target into a delayed damage effect that triggers after 15 turns or after the 10 turn cooldown you can attempt to further enhance this effect up to 6 times and cause devastating damage.

Blood Magic Sacrifice health for damage.

Life Tap Leach health from the target, receive a Heal for the damage done, and apply 2 stacks of ExposeWeakness for 4 turns. If the target has MagicVulnerability apply PoisonVulnerability for 5 turns.

Blood Ritual Sacrifice 50% health from your entire Party to summon a shade of death that moves towards the target. Once reached it will infect the targets soul with a crippling DoT that also applies 10 stacks of ExposeWeakness, MagicVulnerability, and Frailty for 5 turns.
